Non-Emergency Medical Transportation, Done Right

Many seniors and patients rely on random rideshare drivers for critical medical appointments. Different drivers, different cars, and uncertain timing can create unnecessary stress.

Recurring Appointments

Designed specifically for ongoing care like dialysis, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, physical therapy, infusion visits, lab work, and post-operative follow-ups.

Consistency & Commitment

The same driver whenever possible. Familiar routines. A real sense of accountability for showing up on time.

Clear Communication

Simple coordination with patients and families. Confirmations, expectations, and respectful assistance.

Why I Started Care Mobility

I started Care Mobility after noticing how many seniors and patients with recurring health appointments were relying on random rideshare drivers. New driver every time, unpredictable arrivals, and no real commitment.

For treatments like dialysis and chemotherapy, reliability is not optional. Care Mobility is built around showing up on time, every time, for people who depend on it.
