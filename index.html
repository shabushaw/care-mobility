Why Rideshare Isn‚Äôt Built for Recurring Medical Care

Services like Uber and Lyft can be convenient and lower cost for everyday transportation. But for recurring medical appointments with strict schedules, rideshare options often fall short.

Reliability

Rideshare availability depends on driver supply at the moment a ride is requested. For time-sensitive medical appointments, this uncertainty can create stress or missed care.

Punctuality

Appointment pickups require precision. Rideshare ETAs can change due to traffic, cancellations, or last-minute driver reassignment.

Different Drivers Every Time

Each trip may involve a new driver unfamiliar with the patient, routine, or clinic location.

Different Vehicles

Vehicle size, comfort, and ease of entry vary from ride to ride, which matters for patients with mobility challenges.

Lower Cost, Lower Consistency

Rideshare may be less expensive for one-off trips, but that lower price often comes with less predictability and accountability over time.
