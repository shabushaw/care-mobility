Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the greater Las Vegas area and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on distance, frequency, and specific needs. We offer transparent, flat-rate pricing with no surprises. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your situation.

Do you accept insurance or Medicare?

We can provide documentation for insurance reimbursement where applicable. While we don't bill insurance directly, many clients successfully submit our invoices for partial reimbursement through their health plans.

What if my appointment time changes?

We understand medical appointments can shift. We offer flexible rescheduling with as much notice as possible. Our goal is to adapt to your care needs.

Can I have the same driver for all my appointments?

Yes! Consistency is core to our service. We match you with a dedicated driver who learns your preferences and becomes a familiar, trusted face.

What if I need to bring a companion?

Absolutely. Family members or caregivers are welcome to ride along at no additional charge.
